The Audio Engineering Process

1

Assessment

Detailed analysis of the raw audio to identify areas needing improvement and establish project goals.

2

Editing

Cleaning up recordings, removing noise, correcting timing issues, and preparing tracks for processing.

3

Processing

Applying EQ, compression, effects, and spatial processing to enhance the audio quality.

4

Mixing

Balancing all elements, creating depth and dimension, and ensuring clarity across all playback systems.

5

Mastering

Final polish to achieve commercial loudness and ensure translation across all listening environments.
