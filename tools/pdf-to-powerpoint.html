Frequently Asked Questions

How does the PDF to PowerPoint converter work?

Our PDF to PowerPoint converter analyzes your PDF document, extracts text, images, and formatting, then recreates the content as editable PowerPoint slides. The process preserves layouts, fonts, colors, and images to create a presentation that looks like your original PDF but is fully editable in PowerPoint.

Is my PDF document secure during the conversion process?

Yes, your document is completely secure. Our conversion happens entirely in your browser - your files never leave your computer. We don't store, share, or access your files in any way. After conversion, all temporary files are automatically deleted from your browser's memory.

What types of content can be converted from PDF to PowerPoint?

Our converter handles text, images, tables, charts, shapes, and basic formatting. Complex elements like animations, videos, or interactive forms in the PDF will be converted into static elements or images. The quality of conversion depends on the structure and complexity of your original PDF.

What is the maximum file size for conversion?

Our free service supports PDF files up to 25MB in size. This limit ensures optimal performance and conversion speed. For larger files, we recommend splitting your PDF into smaller parts before conversion or using our premium service which supports larger file sizes.
