About QR Codes

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of information such as URLs, text, contact details, and more. They can be scanned using a smartphone camera or a QR code reader app.

Tips for QR Codes

  • Keep your data short to make a less complex QR code that's easier to scan
  • Ensure good contrast between the QR code and background colors
  • Higher error correction makes your QR code more reliable but also more complex
  • Always test your QR code with different devices before sharing it
