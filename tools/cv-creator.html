CV Writing Tips

Tailor Your CV

Customize your CV for each job application to highlight relevant skills and experience.

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers and metrics to demonstrate your impact (e.g., "Increased sales by 25%").

Use Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs like "Developed," "Implemented," or "Led."

Keep It Concise

Aim for a 1-2 page CV that focuses on your most relevant experience.

Proofread Carefully

Eliminate spelling and grammar errors that could create a negative impression.

Use Simple Formatting

Clean, consistent formatting makes your CV easy to read and professional.
