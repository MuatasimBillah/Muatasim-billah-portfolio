Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure when using this tool?

Yes, 100% secure. All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your photos never leave your device - we don't upload them to any server.

What file formats are supported?

Our editor supports JPEG, PNG, WebP, and GIF formats. You can download your edited images in JPEG, PNG or WebP formats with customizable quality settings.

Are there any file size limits?

For optimal performance, we recommend files under 10MB. Larger files may be processed slower depending on your device capabilities.

Can I save my edits and continue later?

Currently, the editor doesn't support saving sessions. We recommend completing your edits before leaving the page.
