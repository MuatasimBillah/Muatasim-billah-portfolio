Frequently Asked Questions

Is this voice recorder really free?

Yes, our voice recorder is completely free to use with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or limitations. You can record as many audio files as you need and download them all without any cost.

What happens to my audio recordings?

Your privacy is our priority! All recordings stay entirely on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. Everything is processed locally in your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security for your audio content.

What recording quality does this tool provide?

Our voice recorder captures audio in high-quality 48kHz WAV format with 16-bit depth, providing professional-grade sound. This quality level is suitable for podcasts, interviews, music demos, and other professional applications.

Which browsers are supported?

Our voice recorder works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari (14+), and Opera. For the best experience with audio processing and visualization, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Firefox.

Can I edit my recordings after I make them?

Currently, our tool focuses on high-quality recording with pause/resume functionality. For editing needs, you can download your recordings in WAV format and use our Audio Cutter tool or any other audio editor.
