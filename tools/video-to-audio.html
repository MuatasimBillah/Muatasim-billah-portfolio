Frequently Asked Questions

Is this video to audio converter free to use?

Yes, our video to audio converter is completely free to use with no hidden fees or watermarks. You can convert unlimited videos to audio formats like MP3, WAV, OGG, and M4A without any restrictions on file number or size.

How is my privacy protected when converting videos?

Your privacy is 100% protected as all processing happens directly in your browser. Your video files are never uploaded to any server - the conversion happens locally on your device using advanced browser technologies. This ensures complete confidentiality of your content while still providing high-quality audio extraction.

What video formats can I convert to audio?

Our converter supports all major video formats including MP4, AVI, MOV, WebM, MKV, FLV, WMV, and more. You can convert any of these formats to audio outputs including MP3, WAV, OGG, and M4A with various quality settings to suit your needs.

Can I adjust the audio quality of the converted file?

Yes, you can select different quality levels for your output audio. For MP3, we offer bitrates from 64kbps (smaller file size) up to 320kbps (highest quality). For WAV files, you can choose between different sample rates and bit depths. These options allow you to balance between file size and audio quality based on your specific needs.

Is it possible to trim the audio before converting?

Yes, our tool includes a trimming feature that allows you to select the exact portion of the video you want to extract audio from. You can specify the start and end points using our intuitive waveform interface, which lets you visualize the audio before finalizing the conversion.

Can I convert multiple video files at once?

Yes, our converter supports batch processing. You can upload multiple video files simultaneously and convert them all to your chosen audio format. Each file will be processed individually, and you can download them one by one or all together as a zip file when the conversion is complete.

Will the audio quality be reduced during conversion?

The quality of the extracted audio depends on the quality of the original video source and your selected output settings. Our converter uses high-quality algorithms to preserve the audio fidelity as much as possible. For best results, we recommend using high-quality source videos and selecting appropriate output bitrates/quality settings.
