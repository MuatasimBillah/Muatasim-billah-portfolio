Privacy Policy

Our commitment to your privacy and how we protect your data.

Introduction to Our Privacy Policy

Welcome to Mehar Designer Tools. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any data related to your use of our tools.

Effective Date: May 1, 2023

At Mehar Designer Tools, we've built our services with a fundamental principle: your data belongs to you. Our tools are designed to process information directly in your browser, which means most of your data never leaves your device.

Client-Side Processing

All our tools operate entirely within your browser using client-side processing. This means:

  • Your files never leave your device or get uploaded to our servers
  • Processing happens locally on your computer using your browser's capabilities
  • We do not have access to your uploaded files, created content, or recordings
  • Your work remains private and under your control at all times

For example, when you use our Voice Recorder tool, the audio is recorded, processed, and stored directly on your device. We never receive or store your recordings on our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect or store:

  • Your audio recordings, images, or any files you process with our tools
  • Personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details
  • Payment information (we don't offer paid services)
  • Your location data
  • Your browsing history outside of our website
  • Any data from your device's microphone, camera, or other sensors beyond the immediate use required for tool functionality

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal browser storage technologies to improve your experience:

Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your preferences and settings, such as:

  • Dark/light theme preference
  • Tool-specific settings (e.g., color palette preferences)
  • Metadata about your recordings or created content (the actual content remains on your device)

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us.

Optional Analytics Cookies

We may use basic analytics to understand how our tools are used so we can improve them. If implemented, these analytics:

  • Collect anonymized usage data (like page views and tool interactions)
  • Do not identify you personally
  • Can be disabled through your browser settings

You can clear all stored browser data at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Our website incorporates a few third-party services for functionality:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Font Awesome Icons for user interface None
Google Fonts Web fonts for typography None
CDN Services Delivery of static resources None

These services may collect standard technical information (IP address, browser type) as part of their operation. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Data Security

While we do not collect or store your personal data on our servers, we take these steps to ensure security:

  • We use HTTPS to encrypt all data transferred between your device and our servers
  • We regularly update our website and tools to address any security vulnerabilities
  • We design our tools to minimize security risks during client-side processing

Since data processing happens on your device, you should also take precautions to ensure your device and browser are secure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to remove such information.

Your Rights

Because we don't collect or store your personal data on our servers, there's typically no data for you to access, delete, or modify on our end. However, you retain these rights:

  • The right to clear your browser's local storage and cookies
  • The right to use our tools without providing personal information
  • The right to contact us with privacy concerns or questions

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
