Frequently Asked Questions

Which audio file formats are supported?

Our audio cutter supports MP3, WAV, OGG, and M4A formats. The tool automatically detects the format of your uploaded file and preserves the same format for the output file, ensuring no quality loss during the process.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, you can upload audio files up to 100MB in size. This limit is in place to ensure optimal performance in your browser. For larger files, we recommend splitting them into smaller chunks first or using a file compression tool before uploading.

Does this tool reduce audio quality?

No, our audio cutter performs a lossless cut of your original file. This means the selected portion maintains exactly the same quality as the source file. We don't re-encode the audio during the cutting process, which preserves the original bitrate and quality.

Is this tool completely free to use?

Yes, our audio cutter is completely free with no hidden fees, no account creation requirements, and no usage limitations. You can cut and download as many audio files as you need without any restrictions.

Is my audio data secure when using this tool?

Absolutely. Our audio cutter works entirely within your browser using JavaScript. Your audio files are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere in the cloud. This ensures complete privacy and security for all your audio files.
