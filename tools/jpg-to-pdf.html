How many images can I convert at once?

You can convert up to 20 JPG/JPEG images at once, with each image being up to 5MB in size. This limit is in place to ensure optimal performance in the browser. For most users, this is more than sufficient for creating multi-page PDF documents.

What's the difference between the quality settings?

The quality settings affect the compression level of images in your PDF. "High" preserves most details and is ideal for photos and detailed images but results in larger file sizes. "Medium" provides a good balance between quality and file size. "Low" applies more compression, reducing file size significantly but may affect image clarity.

Currently, this specific tool is optimized for JPG/JPEG formats. However, most devices allow you to save or export other image formats (like PNG or BMP) as JPG before uploading. For direct conversion of other formats, check out our other conversion tools available in our tools collection.
