7. Cookies & Tracking

My website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content.

7.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

7.2 Types of Cookies Used

I use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be turned off.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These help me to understand how visitors interact with my website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.
  • Functionality Cookies: These enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences.

7.3 Third-Party Cookies

My website may also use third-party services that may set their own cookies, such as Google Analytics. These third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies.

7.4 Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of my website.
