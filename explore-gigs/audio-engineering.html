Frequently Asked Questions

What audio formats do you accept?
I accept all major audio formats including WAV, AIFF, MP3, FLAC, and even stems/multitrack sessions from any DAW. For best results, please provide uncompressed audio files (WAV/AIFF) at the highest resolution you have (24-bit/48kHz or higher).
How many revisions are included?
Basic package includes 2 revisions, Standard includes 5 revisions, and Premium includes unlimited revisions. I want you to be completely satisfied with the final result, so we'll work together until it's perfect.
Do you provide mixing and mastering separately?
Yes! You can order mixing only, mastering only, or both services together. For the best results and value, I recommend combining both services, but I can work with your specific needs and budget.
What's your turnaround time?
Standard delivery is 2-3 days for most projects. Rush delivery options are available for an additional fee. Complex projects with many tracks may require additional time, which will be discussed before starting.
Can you work with reference tracks?
Absolutely! Please provide 2-3 reference tracks that represent the sound you're aiming for. This helps me understand your vision and deliver results that match your expectations and artistic direction.
